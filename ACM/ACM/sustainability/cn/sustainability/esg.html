ACM ESG

ACM STYLE

Respect cultural diversity

ACM Entertainment is based on the "ACM STYLE" that respects cultural diversity. Dedicated to becoming a leader in the domestic entertainment industry, surpassing South Korea to become the world's number one music and entertainment company, and realizing ESG value in this growth process.

Especially in all business operations, including music and various media content, the company will establish an effective ESG management system and make every effort to contribute to the sustainability of society and the environment.

ESG Objectives

Impact Beyond Creativity

  • Strengthening ethical and moral management
  • Earned Value Management

  • Responsible business management

    Responsible Managment

    We will become an honest, open, and transparent enterprise.

  • Creating environmental value

    Inspire Green Entertainment

    We will contribute to creating environmental value for the next generation.

  • Spread the value of inclusivity

    Empower Every Voice

    We will work hand in hand with everyone to lead the dissemination of inclusive values.

ESG organizational structure​

ACM Entertainment has established a dedicated sustainable development management team under the CEO and formed an ESG business coordination team with relevant department business personnel to formulate and implement specific tasks for ESG promotion.

Policies and Guidelines

  • Human rights policy

    ACM Entertainment attaches great importance to the impact of the company's business on human rights and respects and protects the human rights of customers, artists, employees and partners.

  • Moral rules

    ACM Entertainment and ACM Entertainment employees are responsible to investors, shareholders, customers, artists and employees.

  • Industrial Safety Management Policy

    ACM Entertainment regards safety management as its highest priority value and leads a safe and healthy entertainment culture.

  • Diversity policy

    ACM Entertainment respects the diversity of customers, artists, employees and partners and does not treat them differently.

  • Environmental policy

    ACM Entertainment values the impact of its business on the environment.

  • Anti-Corruption Compliance Policy

    ACM Entertainment develops anti-corruption compliance policies to better serve customers and the market.

  • Fair trading policy

    ACM Entertainment adheres to fair and equitable transactions and strives to comply with antitrust laws and fair trade regulations.

  • Partner Code of Conduct

    ACM Entertainment respects ethics and human rights and strives for common development with its partners.
